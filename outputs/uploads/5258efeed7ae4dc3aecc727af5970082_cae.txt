What Will You Do?



In this role, you'll design and implement SCADA systems tailored to Offshore Substation projects. It’s a position that blends system architecture, integration, and cross-disciplinary collaboration.

Design SCADA systems: architecture, functional specs, and control philosophies
Specify and integrate both hardware and software for robust, responsive systems
Apply industrial communication protocols like IEC 61850, Modbus, and OPC
Produce clear technical documentation: block diagrams, interface specs, design reports
Collaborate with electrical, automation, and telecom engineers to ensure system compatibility
Ensure compliance with industry standards, safety regulations, and cybersecurity best practices
